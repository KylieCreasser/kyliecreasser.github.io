Hi, I’m Kylie.

Marketing student focused on campaigns, content, and execution—with an interest in analytics, collaboration, and experience-driven work.

My Story

I got into marketing because I like understanding how things actually work—how campaigns are built, how messaging evolves, and what makes people take action. While most of my experience has been in content creation, I’m especially interested in the strategy and analytics behind it and how everything connects.

As a Digital Marketing and AI major with minors in AI and Economics, I’ve been able to explore both the creative and technical sides of marketing. I’ve worked with programming languages like Java, Python, and C#, which has shaped how I approach problem-solving and think through more efficient, data-driven decisions.

Through my internships, I’ve worked on multi-phase campaigns, short-form video, email marketing, and cross-campus collaborations. At the Career Center, I regularly meet with different organizations to plan collaborative content and promote events and resources in ways that feel relevant and engaging to students. In my SECU internship, I built two full campaigns—one student-facing and one employer-facing—each structured across awareness, application, and reminder phases.

Outside of that, I’m someone who enjoys being part of creative and energetic environments. I’ve always had a strong connection to music—I love discovering new artists, going to small shows, and attending larger events. It’s shaped how I think about experiences and connection, and it’s a big part of why I’m drawn to work that brings people together.

I’m also currently working with a team to develop a video game called RoboHospital, which has been a fun way to combine creativity, technology, and collaboration. Alongside that, I’ve been involved in the Ski and Snowboard Club throughout college, competing in USCSA races and serving as Event Coordinator. In that role, I planned travel, coordinated with resorts, managed logistics, and led fundraising efforts that brought in over $3,000 for our team.

What I enjoy most, though, is working with people. I like taking ideas and turning them into something real, and I enjoy being part of a team that collaborates well and builds something together. I value positive, supportive environments and try to contribute to that wherever I am.

Throughout my internships, I’ve really valued building relationships with the people around me and learning from their experiences. I try to absorb as much as I can and grow from what I’m exposed to.

I’m excited to be part of a team where I can continue learning, contribute meaningfully, and grow alongside others. I’ll be relocating to Orlando, Florida in July and am actively seeking opportunities in marketing, campaigns, and digital strategy.
