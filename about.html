Hi, I’m Kylie.

I got into marketing through a mix of creativity and structure—I like understanding how campaigns are built, how messaging evolves, and what actually makes people take action. At the UNC Charlotte Career Center, I create content that promotes events and resources, but a big part of my role is also collaboration. I meet with different organizations across campus, identify opportunities to work together, and develop content that aligns with current trends and student behavior. In my SECU internship, I focused more heavily on campaign strategy and execution. I built two full campaigns—one student-facing and one employer-facing—each structured in three phases: awareness, application, and reminder. This included social content, print assets, and email marketing designed to move audiences from initial interest to action. I’m especially interested in roles that combine strategy and execution—where I can be part of a team building campaigns, working across platforms, and contributing to something bigger than just one piece of content.

My Story

I’m someone who genuinely enjoys fast-paced, creative environments where no two days look the same. I’m drawn to marketing that feels lively, brings people together, and creates a real connection with its audience.

Over the past few years, I’ve built experience in content creation, campaign planning, and brand storytelling through roles that blend creativity with strategy. I love creating content that feels cohesive and on-brand, but I also enjoy looking at the numbers behind it. Paying attention to analytics, timing, and performance is just as interesting to me as the creative side.

At UNC Charlotte, I competed on the Ski and Snowboard Team while also serving as an event coordinator and helping lead fundraising efforts. That experience taught me how to stay organized, communicate clearly, and keep momentum in high-energy environments.

A lot of how I approach work comes from growing up around a family-run business, where I saw how much reliability, work ethic, and genuine connection shape the way people experience a brand. It taught me the importance of being dependable, listening well, and building trust over time.

I care a lot about the people I work with and the energy a team creates together. After graduation, I’ll be relocating to Orlando, Florida, and I’m excited to keep building in creative, people-focused marketing environments.
