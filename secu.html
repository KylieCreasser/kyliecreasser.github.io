SECU Public Fellows Campaign

A multi-phase digital marketing campaign for the SECU Public Fellows Internship Program at UNC Charlotte designed to increase awareness, drive applications, and improve student engagement through structured messaging and cross-platform execution.

Planning Content Student Stories

Overview

Role & Scope

I planned and executed two multi-phase digital marketing campaigns for the SECU Public Fellows Program, one targeting UNC Charlotte students and one targeting employer partners. Each campaign followed a structured funnel approach (awareness → application → conversion), with messaging adapted to each stage.

Focus Areas

  • Campaign Strategy
  • Content Creation
  • Student Spotlights
  • Print & Digital Assets

Platforms

  • Instagram
  • LCD Screens
  • Email + Web
  • Print Materials

Strategy

The campaign followed a phased digital strategy designed to guide users from initial awareness to completed application. Messaging evolved from program introduction to urgency-based conversion, supported by authentic student storytelling to increase credibility and engagement. A parallel campaign targeted employer partners, using tailored messaging and email outreach to communicate program value and drive participation. Campaign decisions were guided by audience behavior and engagement patterns, ensuring messaging and content delivery aligned with each stage of the user journey.

Audience

UNC Charlotte students exploring internship opportunities.

Goal

Increase awareness, clarify program value, and drive completed applications.

Approach

Structured timeline + cross-platform content deployment.

RESULTS & IMPACT

Results & Impact

This campaign increased visibility of the SECU Public Fellows Program through phased, cross-platform messaging and a clearer path from awareness to application.

1,000+ Students Reached

Campaign content reached over 1,000 students across digital and campus-facing touchpoints.

Stronger Program Awareness

Multi-phase messaging helped introduce the internship program clearly and build early interest.

Clearer Application Journey

Structured awareness, application, and reminder content made the process easier to understand and follow.

Cross-Platform Consistency

Messaging and visuals stayed aligned across social, email, print, and digital signage.

Campaign Breakdown

Phase 1 — Awareness

Awareness-focused content introducing the program, highlighting value, and building initial interest.

Phase 2 — Application Push

Deadline-driven messaging focused on getting students to start and submit applications.

Intern Spotlight Content

To humanize the SECU Fellows Program, I developed a multi-format intern spotlight series combining branded graphics with short-form “Day in the Life” video content. This approach increased relatability and helped prospective applicants better understand real internship experiences and outcomes.
